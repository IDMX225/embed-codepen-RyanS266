Pen: Very Simple Slider

This is a basic slider template, perfect for learning how sliders work with minimal code. It's easy to expand upon with transitions, animations, swipe support, and enhanced accessibility. A basic slider is used to display multiple pieces of content (like images, text, or products) in a small space. It makes websites more interactive, saves space, and grabs user attention.
